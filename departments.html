His Majesty King Abdullah II

Agree on a mechanism to pay hospital debts to Libyans

Amman - Under direct directions from His Majesty King Abdullah II to the government, and a close follow-up with Libyan officials, an agreement was reached on a mechanism to pay the debts due to hospitals to the Libyan brothers, within the conditions agreed upon between the two parties. His Majesty ordered that the amounts owed directly from the banks be transferred to hospitals that provided medical and treatment care to Libyan citizens.
